## üéØ Optimization Tips

### App Store Optimization (ASO)

**Keywords**:
- Research competitors
- Use tools like App Annie, Sensor Tower
- Focus on long-tail keywords
- Include job-specific terms (scaffolding, construction)

**Description**:
- Front-load important keywords
- Use bullet points for readability
- Highlight unique features (AI-powered)
- Include social proof once you have reviews

**Screenshots**:
- First screenshot is most important
- Use captions to explain features
- Show results, not just features
- Update seasonally if relevant

**Reviews**:
- Encourage happy users to leave reviews
- Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)
- Address issues mentioned in negative reviews
- Use review prompts in-app (but not too often)

# App Icons

Place your Forge app icons in this directory.

## Required Icon Sizes

- icon-72x72.png
- icon-96x96.png
- icon-128x128.png
- icon-144x144.png
- icon-152x152.png
- icon-192x192.png
- icon-384x384.png
- icon-512x512.png
- icon-1024x1024.png (for iOS App Store)

## Icon Design Guidelines

**Design**: Bold letter "F" in a minimalist, industrial style

**Style Options**:
1. Black "F" on white background (recommended)
2. White "F" on black background
3. Consider anvil/forge imagery as alternative

**Requirements**:
- Simple and recognizable at all sizes
- Professional, industrial aesthetic
- High contrast for visibility
- No gradients (solid colors only)
- PNG format with transparency for sizes < 1024x1024
- PNG format without transparency for 1024x1024 (iOS requirement)
